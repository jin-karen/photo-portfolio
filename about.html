about me

Sated Craving was founded in November 2019 as a creative outlet to showcase the beauty and charm of food. It was a way to document food adventures for kj's own memories and to share with others. The instagram account was founded as eatandrepeat_adv and rebranded in March of 2022 to satedcraving to represent kj's ever-increasing passion for food, each craving sated with every new experience. Sated Craving is currently based in New York City but dedicates itself to maintaining a global perspective and exhibiting unbounded displays.
